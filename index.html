About the Game

In Stonecurrent, each level presents a carved stone structure with incomplete or misaligned channels.

Players must:

The objective is to guide the current from its source to sacred endpoints without overflow or interruption. We wanted to mix that organic, natural look with puzzle mechanics that actually make sense.

  • Rotate stone blocks

  • Slide terrain slabs

  • Open sealed gates

  • Redirect elemental streams

How to Play

  • Study the Terrain

    Look at the stone plateau and understand the layout.
    Find where the current begins and where it must reach.

  • Shape the Path

    Move and rotate stone segments to create a continuous channel.

  • Activate the Flow

    Flip the switch and watch the current travel through the carved paths.

  • Refine the Structure

    If the current breaks, adjust the stone layout and try again.

Advanced Challenges

  • Split currents that power multiple paths

  • Pressure thresholds that require stronger flow

  • Timed gates that open and close

  • Multi-layer platforms with vertical connections

Features

Everything we built ties back to that core idea of manipulating these ancient stone systems.

  • Control the Flow

    Guide the living current through carved channels and branching paths to power the ancient structures.

Uniqueness

Playing it feels like you're working with something that's been around forever, and every move matters.

Unlike standard pipe-connection puzzles:

  • Terrain feels heavy and physical

  • Channels are carved, not mechanical

  • Flow reacts to terrain elevation

  • Visual transformation occurs when endpoints activate

Benefits

It'll definitely make you think through problems step by step. It's not a rush job — you can take your time and really think things through.
